About us

Spindler Brothers is a full-service website design agency based in Southern California. Established in November 2020, cofounders (and brothers) Alex and David believe the end user experience is of the utmost importance. Alex graduated with a degree in Computer Science from UC San Diego and David gradutated with a degree in Mathematics from Westmont College in Santa Barbara. Spindler Brothers is eager to get your next website up and running!
